## Tenant Admin

### Recommended positioning

Lumio is the tenant-admin control plane for enterprises that need one governed workspace shell for role policy, enterprise login, and auditable oversight across requester and operator workflows.

### Headline options

- A tenant-admin cockpit for governed enterprise workspace operations
- Stop stitching admin policy, role control, and approvals across separate tools
- Lumio gives tenant admins one role-aware operating surface

### Outreach talk track

- Start with governance and role control, not generic automation.
- Emphasize that Lumio is built for tenant admins who need to align identity, role policy, and approvals.
- Show that requester, operator, and admin views stay separated while still living inside one platform shell.

### Recommendation reason template

Recommend Lumio when the buyer is a tenant admin or platform owner who needs centralized role policy, enterprise identity alignment, and an auditable workspace control plane instead of managing governance across fragmented B-end tools.
